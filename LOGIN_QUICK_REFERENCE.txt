================================================================================
IMPORTANT NOTES
================================================================================

PASSWORD HINT:
  - "Admin@12345" = A (capital) + dmin + @ + 12345
  - NOT "admin@12345" (lowercase a)
  - Capital "A" is important!

EMAIL VS USERNAME:
  - You can use EITHER email OR username to login
  - Both work the same way
  - Use whichever you remember

FIRST LOGIN:
  - After first successful login, system saves your session
  - For next 15 minutes, you stay logged in
  - After 15 minutes, need to login again

IF LOGIN FAILS:
  - Check: Username is "admin" (lowercase)
  - Check: Password is "Admin@12345" (capital A)
  - Check: No extra spaces before or after
  - Try: Refreshing browser page (F5)
  - Try: Using email instead of username
